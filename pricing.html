Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes you can. Just click on your settings in the dashboard and you’ll be able to see new options.

Can I get a refund?

If you decide you would like to cancel your account, you will not be charged once the month is complete; but you are still responsible for whatever outstanding charges you already have.

Is my data secure?

The security of your personal information is important to us. This information, such as your credit card number on our registration form, will be encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SSL) and your trust will be a priority to all those at On the Run.

Can I reset my password or get a reminder if I forget what it is?

We understand that sometimes staying organized is hard for those who are on-the-go. For this reason, under the login prompt, we offer an area for if you have forgotten your password or username. Enter your email address to send yourself your old password and the option to change it.

Is On the Run available in other languages?

Yes it is. We offer our program in numerous languages; just go to your settings once you’ve created your account and adjust the language setting to your preference.

What are the technical requirements for using On the Run?

On the Run is available anywhere you have connection to the internet or with the offline mode set up. There’s nothing to install, making our program more easily accessible.
